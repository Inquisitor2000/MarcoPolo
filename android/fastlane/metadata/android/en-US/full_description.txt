Find your friends and family in crowded places — no Google Play Services required.

One person creates a session (Marco), the other joins with a 4-character code (Polo). Both see each other on a real-time map with a walking route and turn-by-turn navigation.

Features:
• Real-time map with free CartoDB tiles — no API key needed
• Turn-by-turn navigation with street names and distance arrows
• Two routing modes: foot (pedestrian paths) or car (streets)
• 15-minute session timer
• Multi-language: English, Romanian, Russian
• Fully offline-capable maps (osmdroid)
• No Google Play Services — works on any Android device

Perfect for festivals, concerts, shopping malls, amusement parks, or any crowded space where finding someone is hard.
